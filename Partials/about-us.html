About us
Since 1999 report it® has provided hotline-reporting services for Public and Private companies, Nonprofit Entities, as well as Healthcare and Financial Institutions. Millions of users rely on report it® to securely communicate their concerns directly to senior level management in a confidential and anonymous manner.
The report it® App expands beyond our traditional offerings and takes the lead in an emerging new market segment, Consumer Engagement and Relations. report it® provides you a direct voice to the management of businesses and organizations. Let’s face it, there are many situations in which we all want to “Speak to the Manager”, but few of us actually do because of time constraints or the reality that the “Manager” no authority or interest in elevating our concerns to the proper level of management. Out of frustration we will often turn to Social Media such as Twitter, Instagram, Yelp or Facebook and post our experience. With report it® anyone can seeit, sayit, reportit instantly and anonymously.
All information, including your identity sent via the report it® App is kept anonymous and confidential and you can rest assured that your concern or comment is quickly and efficiently sent to the responsible person at the organization you wish to contact.
